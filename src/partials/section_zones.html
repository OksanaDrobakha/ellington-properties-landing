• Lobby

    Distinctly modern, the lobby at Ellington House is an artsy, edgy space with an understated elegance. High floor-to-ceiling windows frame the lush greenery of outside while acting as entryways for the beautiful natural light to accentuate the soft color palette of the lobby. 

  • Poll deck

    It’s almost as if the pool deck at Ellington House was designed to bring resort-style entertainment to its residents, with an illuminated infinity lap pool providing panoramic views of the iconic Dubai Hills Golf Course. 

  • Fitness center

    The fitness center at Ellington House goes beyond the expected standards of fitness by catering not only to the varied fitness needs of its residents, but also considers their moods, emotions, and habits.
